Refresh Date Table



  • Power BI Desktop ribbon, Click on Transform data.
  • Click the New Source button and select Blank query from the list.
  • On the Dax formula bar, type =Date Time.LocalNow() 
  • Under properties in the Name box, type in Refresh Date
  • Click on the Convert to Table button. 
  • Refresh table pops up, rename the column and choosing to rename.
  • Type Date Time




  • Click on the Close & Apply button and save
  •  Drag and connect the Date column of the STD table with that of the Calendar Table
