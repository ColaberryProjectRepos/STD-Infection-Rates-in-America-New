Create Year Slicer:


1. Next up is our slider for our year. In order to create this, first select a "Slicer Visual" from the visualizations tab. Next, follow the first screenshot and select the "Year" value from our "Date" column in the "Main Dataset." (The second GIF is a brief showcase of the formatting needed to make your slider look good) (For the blue color use the "Main Blue" from the "Project Colors" file in the "Power BI Project" ZIP)





1.        Double click on the Slicer Icon On the visualization bar.

2.        Slicer visual will display on the report.

3.        Drag and drop the Year column from the calendar table.

4.        Click on the drop-down button from the title header 

5.        Select Drop down Option of the year slicer and choose in Between

6.        Turn the header: Off Change the Items Font color black Font Family Arial Black Font Size: 10.

7.        Turn the title: On

8.        8. Type "Year".

9.        9.  Turn on the border. Color: White, 10% darker radius:10.
