Create DAX Measures:

To create measures in the ‘All Measures’ table, Click on the ‘All Measures’ table and click on ‘New measure’,  a Measure window will open, and write the DAX Formula for that Measure as shown in the Gif. Add all the measures one by one by clicking on 'New Measure', paste the formula and click enter or check the tick sign to save.

1.    Click your "Measures Table" and click on "New Measure" at the top. Here we will make our main STD Cases measure. In this measure, you will type the following DAX code:

Ø  Total Cases = SUM('STD Cases'[STD Cases])

Ø  Rate Per 100K = ('STD Cases'[otal Cases] / 'STD Cases'[TotalPopulationRatePer100])*10000

Ø  Female = DIVIDE([Female], 'STD Cases'[TotalPopulationRatePer100])

Ø  Male = DIVIDE([Males], 'STD Cases'[TotalPopulationRatePer100])

Ø  Infection Rate = DIVIDE([Total Cases],[TotalPopulation]) * 100

Ø  Infection YoY = (DIVIDE([Total Cases], [STD Case LY],0)-1)


2. At this point, repeat steps 1-4 except replace the names for the values; Infection Rate, Infection YoY, Rate Per 100k. Copy and paste the DAX codes and replace them with the corresponding values.

 

3. Lastly, we need to get our Year over Year measure to determine the difference between last year's and the current year's STD Infection. In this measure, you will type the following DAX code: Infection YoY = (DIVIDE([.Total Cases], [.STD Case LY],0)-1)

 

4. Next, we will make our icon, YoY, and Rate color measures. These will be used to show an arrow for up and down movements in our YoY and Rate data, and the color will be used for this arrow to show good and bad values.
Color DAX code: YoY and Rate Color =

Arrow Rate = SWITCH(TRUE(), [Infection Rate] < 0, UNICHAR(9660), UNICHAR(9650))

Arrow YoY = SWITCH(TRUE(), [Infection YoY] < 0, UNICHAR(9660), UNICHAR(9650))
